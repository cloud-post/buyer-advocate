Key reasons why you should always engage a buyers’ advocate

Having a professional negotiator focused on your best interests

Some buyers prefer to do everything on their own, but working with a buyers' advocate gives you a significant advantage as you have specialists on your side who have the duty to protect your interests only. If you deal with the listing agent directly, don't forget that they are required to represent the listed vendor's interests alone. While they may appear very friendly, only they have an obligation to present the interests of the listed vendor.

Avoid buying the wrong asset

Save time and stress less

Buying a house is an immense expenditure, and whether you are a very first homeowner or have a great deal of experience, purchasing property can be emotionally stressful for most.
